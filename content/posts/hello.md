This is the first post on the newly rebuilt tksh.io.

The site is built with Next.js and styled to look like a terminal. Every heading uses markdown-style prefixes, navigation uses bracket notation, and the whole thing runs on a monospace font.

## Why a terminal theme?

Terminals are honest interfaces. No flashy animations, no distracting gradients — just text, clearly presented. That's the aesthetic I wanted for a personal site.

## What's here

* Blog posts (you're reading one)
* A resume
* Books I've read and want to read
* Music I like
* Side projects
* An ASCII map of places I've been
